Frequently Asked Questions & Answers

  • Do I need a separate account for home and work?
    • No. One account covers all your devices both in the home and in the office.
  • How many devices are covered?
    • You’re covered for an UNLIMTED number of devices!.
  • Can I cancel my membership if I no longer have any devices that need coverage?
    • Yes. Membership refunds will be prorated based the length of coverage used. See our cancellation and refund polcies.
  • How often can I request support?
    • Remote requests are UNLIMITED!
  • Does it matter if I have a PC or a MAC?
    • No. All types of devices are covered. Even if you have both types, they’re all covered under one membership.
